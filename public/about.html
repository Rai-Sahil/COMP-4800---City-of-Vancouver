About City Of Vancouver Mural Roster:
The City of Vancouver Mural Roster is a list of artists who have been pre-qualified to create murals on city property. The roster is used to streamline the artist selection process for public art projects.
The roster is open to artists who have demonstrated experience in creating murals and public art. Artists are selected through a review process. The roster is continuously updated.
The roster is used by the City of Vancouver and other organizations to select artists for mural projects. The roster is also used by property owners to find artists for private mural projects.

About City of Vancouver Public Art Program: The City of Vancouver Public Art Program commissions artists to create public art that reflects the city’s communities, cultures and history. The program also manages the acquisition and maintenance of the city’s public art collection.

The Public Art Program is part of the City’s Cultural Services department. The program works with other City departments, community groups and private developers to integrate public art into the city’s public spaces.

About CityStudio Vancouver: CityStudio is an innovation hub inside City Hall where staff, students and community co-create experimental projects to make Vancouver more sustainable, liveable and joyful.
CityStudio is a non-profit organization that started in Vancouver and has spread to 10 cities across Canada and Europe. CityStudio Vancouver is a partnership between the City of Vancouver and the city’s six public post-secondary institutions.

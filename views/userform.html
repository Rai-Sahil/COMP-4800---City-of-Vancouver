Artist Roster Application Questions

This is a legal agreement for collection and publishing of artists' information and the publication of their artwork/images for non-commercial use.

Your application will stay active for 2 years, after which time you will need to renew. You will receive an automated e-mail close to the time of expiry and be prompted to renew. If you do not renew, it will expire.

You will be able to edit and/or make changes to your application materials any time after you apply, however, in order for the changes to be published, the City of Vancouver will need to review and approve.

Please provide contact information which you don’t mind being posted publicly. These will be shared on our website in order for interested businesses and organisations to contact you. City staff may also contact you about opportunities.











Do you reside in BC and have the legal right to work here?*


If you are not a BC resident or not legal to work, you can not apply for Artist Roster program.

Do you have past experience producing murals or digital designs?*


If you do not have experience, you can not apply for Artist Roster program.
